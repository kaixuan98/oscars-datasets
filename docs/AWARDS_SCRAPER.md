# Awards Scrapers

One of the factors that add weight to the Oscar best picture winners is the precursor or the forerunners. They are the films where created buzz by winning different awards during the festival and awards seasons. Winnings and nomination will also be used by the distributors in FYC marketing. 

## How does the scraper structured?
Since we will be looking at different type of awards, each awards has a different type for best picture category, so it is structure in Strategy design pattern. 

## What are the awards we will be looking at?
1. Cannes
2. Venice 
3. Golden Globes 
4. Bafta 
5. SAG 
6. Critical Choice awards
